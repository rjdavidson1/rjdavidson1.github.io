About Rose

Software developer and freelance artist with proven expertise in project management, effective communication, and writing clean, concise code. She is a disciplined self-starter who appreciates new challenges. It is her personal philosophy that code should be as efficient as possible, and consistently researches ways to improve the time and space efficiency of algorithms. Incorporates Agile methodologies in a variety of projects ranging from short-term competitive projects to long-term private projects.

In addition to creating dynamic, user-friendly applications Rose creates art, from large-scale murals to small artistic journal entries. Acrylics are her preferred medium of choice but also appreciates collage, charcoal, and watercolors.

Rose is currently looking for an entry-level or junior-level software developer position that will offer great opportunities for personal and professional development.
